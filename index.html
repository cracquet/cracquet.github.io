Cracquet

Jan 5, 2009

Hello and welcome, It's winter. Chilly outside and friends are there. You want real fun, try Cracquet. Sure, it'll bring smiles and groans to one and all.

Rules, there are some.

  • Willowman and Flinger start on two bases on either side of the entrance
  • Sling the ball directly to the front wall
  • ...

You need

  • racquetball court
  • 2-4 brave cracqueteers
  • tennis ball
  • willow
  • a crowd of onlookers for your spectacular show. optional

Rounds

Played in multiple formats. Varied in duration.

  • Limited – 6 Pitches
  • Classic – 6 Pitches
  • Test – Teams alternate every 6 Pitches until willowmen are out.

*Tie breaker for Classic & Tests - 6 Pitches, team with maximum hits to the front wall wins.
